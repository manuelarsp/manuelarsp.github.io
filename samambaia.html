Oficina Samambaia

a brand that feels like home

The Client

Oficina Samambaia is a multidisciplinary studio based in Recife, Brazil. Their aim is to make their client's houses to feel like real homes.

The brief

Inspired by the house plant (samambaia is bracken, in portuguese) and the life it brings to any room, Karla, the architect behind the studio has a very particular approach to interior design & architecture. She wanted to communicate her human-centered methods bringing her clients closer.

The process

Starting with some benchmarking, the first decision was to escape the obvious path of representing geometric shapes. They were the opposite of our value proposition, that goes beyonde made-to-order furniture. Contemporary, versatile and organic were some of the keywords on the process.

the outcome

This responsive branding system is adaptable to different formats, so it can follow Karla´s creative mind wherever her work guides it. The illustrated bits give all her presentation material and stationery an unexpected contemporary, laid back visual impact, while the classic geometric typography makes it trustworthy and profesional.
