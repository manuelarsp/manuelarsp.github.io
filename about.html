about me

made in Brazil, based in Barcelona

I’m a Graphic Designer and Illustrator from Recife, Brazil currently based in Barcelona. On my free time I love to cook, take sunbaths and prefer to walk rather then driving.

My work is mostly playful and much of the inspiration come from Contemporary Art and nature. I'm a reliable, creative and positive professional, and always reach for relevant, meaningful deliverables.

I’ve always loved to hear & tell stories, and that’s why last year I’ve stepped into UI/UX Design (and I love it just as much as printed media!).

what i value

deadlines, sustainability, work-life balance, female empowerment, human centered experiences, good coffee, good food & good mood.

what i do

Illustration, surface design, branding, digital interface design, art direction & más! If you like my style and want to make enquiries or get a quote, feel free to write me (in English, Português or Español!).
